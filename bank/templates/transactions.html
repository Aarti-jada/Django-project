PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK OF INDIA

Punjab National Bank (PNB), India's first Swadeshi Bank, commenced its operations on April 12, 1895 from Lahore. Post Nationalization of Banks in India in 1969, Govt of India has been the largest shareholder of Punjab National Bank. PNB, in its journey of more than a century, has expanded its presence across India.
